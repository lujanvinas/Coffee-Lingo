Espresso

Straight and simple. A full-flavored, concentrated form of coffee that is served in single or double shots.

Americano

A watered-down single or double espresso, literally.

Latte

Usually two shots of espresso served in a big mug, complete with steamed milk and a bit of foam.

Capuccino

Double shot of espresso, steamed milk and a lot of foam.

Macchiato

"Stained" in Italian. Add a splash of steamed milk to a single or double espresso.
